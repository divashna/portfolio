About Me

I design functional and entertaining experiences for mobile, web, TV and beyond.


Philosophy

I believe the best user experiences are the ones that go unnoticed- the uninterrupted user journeys that are seamless to the human eye. When concepting new product features, reviewing existing user flows or redesigning components of a website, I work tenaciously to ensure users are presented with solutions that are simple, elegant and clear.


Values

I'm a big believer of continuous learning and improvement. I aim to learn something new every day, whether through reading up on developments that affect the world around me, scouring the internet for design inspiration, breaking down the user flows of some of the world's most popular sites or collaborating with developers to better understand technical constraints in order to become a more informed and attentive designer.
