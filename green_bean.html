Green Bean

The challenge of food waste

Context

To address food waste as an agricultural and climate change issue, Green Bean embarked on a mission to deliver an app that empowers Australian businesses and households to track, manage and reduce their food usage and household waste.

My Role

  • UI Designer
  • Team Coordinator

    • Status  IN PRODUCTION

    The Green Bean team is conducting testing with external users to assess the viability of the app as a long-term waste solution and are seeking to add external integrations to more effectively measure food conservation efforts.

    Researching the Problem

    To better understand the needs and attitudes of Australian households and businesses towards reducing food waste, I conducted preliminary research on food conservation and waste in Australia. This research allowed me to better understand why food waste occurs and provided a gateway for designing a feasible solution. The research revealed that:

    • 20% of food purchases by Australians are wasted
    • 1.3m tonnes of food waste are generated in Australia each year
    • Each Australian throws out 300kg of food a year; and
    • 76% of Australians care about food waste

    Defining the Problem

    The next step of the process was to formulate a problem statement which would guide the process of designing and developing the app:

    Australian households and business owners often buy more food than can reasonably be consumed, leading to large amounts of food waste as they pass their use-by dates. The solution should provide a platform where Australian businesses and residents can track their food usage in order to avoid buying an excess amount of produce and, therefore, reduce food waste.

    Sketching Solutions

    I then created several sketches to gain a better understanding of how the final platform would look.

    Three iterations of sketches were created to visualise the style and layout of the features in the app. The sketches were also used to identify which iteration best showcased the different functions in the app.

    Wireframing

    After selecting one iteration of the sketches, I created wireframes of the app to determine the layout of its features and functions. I composed several versions of the wireframes, each of which tested different layouts and shapes to determine the best visual layout.

    Prototyping

    An interactive prototype was developed on Figma using the startup's brand guidelines. The Green Bean app has been optimised for mobile users and is a convenient, easy-to-use app for both households and businesses.

    The app features options for users to track their food waste, create meals based on the ingredients in their pantry and only purchase ingredients as needed, thus avoiding food waste. Food conservation results are further displayed to the user, providing incentives to continually reduce food waste.
